Creative Writing

What is creative writing

creative writing is a way for people to let their thoughts flow on paper and allow them to have an outlet for feelings.

What is it good for

Creative writing allows you to write down your feelings n the form of a story, instructional manual or anything else you want to write about

It allows you to better understand yourself and what you may be going through. This can include writing about thing that you want to tell someone but are too scared to tell them.

Share With A Friend

Once your done, you can either share with a friend or keep it to yourself. It's your self expression you can do anything you want with it.

Writing can relieve stress and engage the mind. It is something that is good not only for adults but for children to do as well. It will increase their knowledge of the structure of writing, increase their scores on spelling tests, and increase their vocabulary.
