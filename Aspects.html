Writing or typing?

When writing on paper, you get to constantly practice your hand writing. Since writing on paper doens't require a screen, you can stay up late writing without the eyestrain that you get from looking at a screen.

When typing on a computer, you can put thoughts into words a lot faster and you can erase any mistake you make. You also have access to tools that can help you with grammar and spelling.
