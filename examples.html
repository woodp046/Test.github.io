The fire in the heart

No matter who you are, there is a fire in your heart. It grows with passion and love to become a power that cannot be stopped bringing creativity and thinking wherever it goes. Pursuing your passion makes this fire grow stronger.

Storm

It was a calm night. The children were sitting by the fire.

"Papa! come play with us"

"I'll be right there." I called

The house started to shake. I opened the shutters to see what was going on outside. The window revealed a great storm of thunder, lightning and wind. The window started to bend inwards. I jumped back as the window shattered. I felt the bits and pieces of glass cut into me as I fell to the floor. I heard the children scream as the walls of the house got lifted into the air by a great tornado.

Then the scream were gone. I looked over to where the children were sitting and they were just gone. I looked up and saw bodies flying through the air, swept up by the tornado. I started to cry. I closed my eyes and let the storm take me.
