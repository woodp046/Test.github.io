Benefits of Creative Writing

Confidence

Creative writing helps you build self confidence by allowing your thoughts and ideas flow onto a paper without fear of ridicule or limitations

Artistic self-expression

Creative writing is a type of art style, you paint a picture in the readers mind. It allows you to be artistic

Creativity

You can write about anything. you can create your own universe. It is one of the most creative activities you can do. Which will allow you to think outside the box This could help if you need to find a creative solution to a problem.

Mental Break

Creative Writing is a mentally relaxing activity because you are allowed to do anything or create anything you want.
